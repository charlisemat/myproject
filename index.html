My name is Charlise Matsuda. I am finishing my first year at Brigham Young University. I want to share some tips to improve your academics and social life for your first year of college.

Here is some advice to help you succeed in your academics:

  • Schedule time in your calendar to do homework
  • Find a good place to study. Avoid studying in your dorm room. Here are my top places I have studied on campus from most productive to least productive:
    1. Library
    2. JSB Lounge
    3. Outside on the grass (when the weather is nice)
    4. My dorm room (I give in sometimes.)
      • There are so many other places. There are also places with study rooms you can reserve.
  • Make your own academic planner with all of your homework assignments, exams, & projects. Plan what assignments you will work on each day on another planner
  • Talk to your TAs. Go to the TA lab for help.
  • Check your grades often on learning suite or canvas
